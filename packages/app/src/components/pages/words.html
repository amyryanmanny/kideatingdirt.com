    This is a dictionary-like resource, used to define some words commonly used by Ryan Manny and surrounding parties.
    To find a word, use CTRL-F
    

    BAKER'S WEEK
        Noun
            1. A length of time measuring eight days.
    

    BUMBERTON
        Noun
            1. Slightly disdainful term for a person.
            2. Endearing term for a person.
            3. False name used to hide your identity at Starbucks or any fast food establishment.
        Exclamation
            4. Phrase to be shouted out of a car window at passersby.
               Means nothing.
    

    BUMBERTON'S DELIGHT
        Noun
            1. A synonym for Barbecue Sauce, specifically the type given out at fast food restaurants.
    

    DAPPERTALISM
        Noun
            1. Alternative class system in which social standing and earnings rely purely on how fancily
               you dress, rather than the other way around.
    

    YOU DINGLE DOG!
        Exclamation
            1. Another exclamation to be shouted out of car windows. 
               Specifically to be shouted at palm trees or other palm-related entites (excl. palm Sunday).
               NOTE: May be a property of Marvel.
    

    I DINGLE DOG DARE YOU!
        Exclamation
            1. An alternative to shouting "You Dingle Dog!," upon passing by a palm-related entity a second time.
    

    VENTNOR
        Noun
            1. A word used to describe someone who violates rules in Monopoly, or who is not paying attention.
